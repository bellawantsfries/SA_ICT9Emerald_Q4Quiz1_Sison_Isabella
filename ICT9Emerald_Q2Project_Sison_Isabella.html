The Philippines, an archipelago jewel cradled in the embrace of the Pacific Ocean, is a vibrant tapestry woven with a rich blend of cultural influences, tropical landscapes, and warm-hearted hospitality. Beneath the azure waters lie kaleidoscopic coral gardens, home to an extraordinary array of marine life. Dive into the depths, and you'll discover a world of technicolor coral reefs, playful dolphins, and gentle giants like the whale shark, creating an underwater haven for enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Here are my top 3 personal favorite places to visit in the Philippines!

Banaue Rice Terraces

  • Welcome to the Banaue Rice Terraces, the heart and soul of the Philippines' highlands. As you approach, the emerald green of the terraces unfolds before your eyes, cascading down the mountains like a living staircase to the heavens. It's as if the mountains themselves are adorned in a finely woven tapestry of rice paddies, a testament to the harmonious dance between human ingenuity and the rugged beauty of the Cordillera Mountains.

Bonifacio High Street - Taguig

  • The City life of the Philippines. Bonifacio High Street is a vibrant and modern commercial and lifestyle district located in Bonifacio Global City (BGC) in Metro Manila, Philippines. It is known for its lively atmosphere, diverse retail and dining options, cultural events, and overall festive ambiance. Public spaces within Bonifacio High Street are often adorned with art installations, sculptures, and greenery, enhancing the visual appeal of the area. These elements contribute to a festive and aesthetically pleasing environment, creating a sense of celebration within the urban landscape.

Cebu City

  • Apart from the grand parade, various religious and cultural events take place, including solemn processions, novena masses, and other activities that showcase the deep-rooted faith of the Cebuanos. The festival is a unique blend of religious devotion and cultural expression, making it a truly captivating experience.
